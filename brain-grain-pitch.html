The Core Insight

Most education systems ask: "What did the student answer?"

We ask: "How does the student think, cope with failure, and engage with peers?"

This shifts measurement from test scores to cognitive capability ‚Äî a layer untouched by existing EdTech.

Brain Grain is a deeptech platform that measures and optimizes how humans think, regulate emotions, and learn ‚Äî transforming invisible cognitive struggles into actionable intelligence before academic failure, workplace mismatch, or mental health crises occur.

The Lifelong Cognition Journey: From School to Work

Age 7‚Äì12: School Years (Pods + Classrooms)

What we observe: Entry behaviour, engagement patterns, how they cope with failure in real-world learning

What we do: Personalized pods (small groups, safe space); share insights with schools

Impact:

  • Early detection of cognitive/emotional struggles (before marks crash)
  • Mentors provide targeted support (regulation strategies, optimal group size, pacing)
  • Child builds foundational resilience and learning confidence
  • Parents understand how their child learns (not just marks)

Outcome: 15‚Äì20% higher engagement, reduced school disengagement signals

Age 12‚Äì18: School-to-College Transition

What happens: Profile travels with student (with consent) to new school/college; teachers see how this student thinks and learns

What we do: Dashboards for teachers/advisors; personalized transition strategies

Impact:

  • Fewer "lost" students in large school transition
  • Teachers adapt teaching/grouping from day 1 (not guessing)
  • Student self-advocacy improves ("I learn best with X")
  • Early identification of anxiety, mismatch, or skill gaps

Outcome: Higher retention across school transitions; 10‚Äì15% fewer disengagements

Age 18‚Äì27: Higher Ed + Workforce

What happens: Profile integrated into college/HR systems; used for course selection, project teams, onboarding

What we do: Early-warning dashboards in LMS/HR; role recommendations; training customization

Impact:

  • Colleges catch at-risk students before dropout (via engagement signals)
  • Employers place people in roles matching their learning/regulation style
  • Training customized (e.g., "this person needs 1:1 feedback, not large group")
  • 20‚Äì30% improvement in course completion, job retention, and performance

Outcome: Higher graduation rates, better job fit, reduced early-career attrition
